The Question

As a human being in the 21st century, I use Yelp to find restaurants, businesses and much more. One day at lunch with a friend, he said "I love yelp but does it have to look so outdated?"

The Answer

Thinking about it, I told him "well, Yelp isn't really for food. It's just a modern day version of Yellow Pages." As I walked home, I asked myself "What if Yelp was actually designed for what people mostly use it as?" From there, I started making some sketches and evaluate what people felt was wrong with the site. The first thing that came to mind was a larger map.
